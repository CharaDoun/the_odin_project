Pesto alla Genovese pasta Recipe

Description

Pesto alla Genovese is a famous Italian sauce originating from Genoa! Its main ingredients are basil, pine nuts, parmesan cheese, olive oil, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 100g basil leaves
  • 100 g olive oil
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 100g pine nuts
  • 100g parmesan cheese, grated
  • 500 g spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Place a frying pan over medium heat, add the pine nuts, and stir constantly with a wooden spoon, for 2-3 minutes, until the pine nuts are nicely golden and toasted.
  2. Remove the pan from the heat and set it aside to cool for 10 minutes.
  3. Put the olive oil, basil leaves, and garlic into a chopper blender, and beat them until completely mashed.
  4. Add the pine nuts, and beat again until they are crushed and incorporated into the mixture.
  5. Add the parmesan and pepper, beat for a few seconds until completely combined, and set the pesto aside.
  6. Place a pot over high heat, fill it ¾ full with water, and bring it to a boil.
  7. Add some salt and the pasta, and boil it according to the packet’s instructions.
  8. Remove the pot from the heat, drain the pasta, and reserve a little of the pasta water.
  9. Transfer the pasta back into the pot, add the pesto and, using a ladle, pour 1 ladleful of the pasta water.
  10. Mix until the pesto is completely combined with the spaghetti, and then divide the pasta among plates.
  11. Serve with basil leaves and freshly ground pepper.
