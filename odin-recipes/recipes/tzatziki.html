Tzatziki Recipe

Description

Traditional greek sauce. The basic ingredients are thick strained yogurt, cucumber, garlic, olive oil, and fresh dill.

Ingredients

  • 300g strained yogurt
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/3 clove of garlic
  • 3 tablespoon vinegar, of white wine
  • 1/4 bunch dill, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the cucumber and grate with a cheese grater, using the large blades.
  2. Put in a bowl. Add a pinch of salt and 1 tablespoon of white wine vinegar. Toss and set aside to release its liquid.
  3. In the meantime, combine the yogurt, 1/3 of a garlic clove (minced), 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar and 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Mix until combined.
  4. Mix until it is combined and creamy.
  5. Squeeze the cucumber with your hands to release any remaining liquid. Discard the liquid and add the cucumber to the yogurt mixture.
  6. Stir into mixture. Add pepper and adjust salt according to taste.
  7. Add some finely chopped dill and your sauce is ready.
  8. Refrigerate until needed.
  9. Serve it with some carrots, peppers, cucumber and Greek pita bread!
